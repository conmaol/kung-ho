# 這

**這** `zhè` ‘this’ is the proximal demonstrative [pronoun](index.md).

## Functions

### Subject

> **這[是](../verbs/sh/shi4.md)冰茶。** `zhè shì bīngchá` ‘This is iced tea.’ \[🦉1.1\]
>
> **這[是](../verbs/sh/shi4.md)美國。** `zhè shì měiguó` ‘This is America.’ \[🦉1.2\]

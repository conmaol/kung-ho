# 你

## 你有...

**你[有](../verbs/有.md)...** `nǐ yǒu ...` ‘you have ...’

> **你有貓嗎** `nǐ yǒu māo ma` ‘Do you have a cat?’

## 你的...

**你[的](../other/的.md)...** `nǐ de ...` ‘your ...’

> **這是你的熱茶** `zhè shì nǐ de rè chá` ‘this is your hot tea’

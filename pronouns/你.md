# 你

**你** `nǐ` ‘you’ is the second-person [pronoun](index.md).

## Functions

### Subject

> **你[有](../verbs/有.md)貓嗎** `nǐ yǒu māo ma` ‘Do you have a cat?’

### Possessive

**你[的](../other/的.md)...** `nǐ de...` ‘your ...’

> **這是你的熱[茶](../nouns/茶.md)** `zhè shì nǐ de rè chá` ‘this is your hot tea’

# 這

## 這是...

**這[是](verbs/是.md)...** `zhè shì ...` ‘this is ...’

> **這是冰茶** `zhè shì bīng chá` ‘this is iced tea’ \[🦉1.1\]
>
> **這是你的水** `zhè shì nǐ de shuǐ` ‘this is your water’ \[🦉1.1\]
>
> **這是美國** `zhè shì měiguó` ‘this is America’ \[🦉1.2\]

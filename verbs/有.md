# 有

**有** `yǒu` ‘have’ is a [verb](index.md).

## Subjects

**[我](../pronouns/我.md)有...** `wǒ yǒu ...` ‘I have ...’

> **我有貓** `wǒ yǒu māo` ‘I have a cat’

**[你](../pronouns/你.md)有...** `nǐ yǒu ...` ‘you have ...’

> **你有狗嗎** `nǐ yǒu gǒu ma` ‘Do you have a dog?’
